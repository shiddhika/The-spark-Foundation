WHO ARE WE?


India is a land of suffering, and its 35 million stray animals are faring even worse. We’re fighting to help the animals no one else will.
More than 35 million stray dogs roam the streets of India, without food, veterinary care or refuge from its often punishing elements. We’re fighting to give them a chance.

India’s animal welfare system struggles with a lack of personnel and capital to build infrastructure to help suffering stray animals.
Help us build a network of stray animal rescues, veterinarians, and animal lovers to give these pets-to-be the critical intervention, veterinary care, and ultimately loving home they deserve.
